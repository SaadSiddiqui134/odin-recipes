THIS IS BIRYANI



Biryani is a staple of Pakistani cuisine which is made of succulent meat, lush spices, flavourful and aromatic rice. The meat is tender due to the Dum style of cooking. In this style of cooking, a first layer of rice is laid in a pot and then the meat is put over the rice. After this, another layer of rice is put on top of the meat. Finally, the pot is sealed and the dish cooks.


Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Spices
  • Yogurt
  • Sliced Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps To Make It

  1. Marinate Meat and Vegitables

  2. Prepare the Rice

  3. Prepare the Fried Onions

  4. Layer the Biryani

  5. Dum Cooking
